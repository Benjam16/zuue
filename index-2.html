Zeus is a character who illustrates Matt Furie in the world of Pepe the Frog. This is evident through Zeus’s distinctive earring, mirroring the one worn by Matt Furie himself.

It has recently come to light that the art of Zeus (Pepe’s Dog) was created by the digital artist ndhaus (@aradew55) on February 7, 2022, as a gift for his supportive friend Jagged (@jaggedsoft).

Ndhaus creatively merges elements from Matt Furie, his art, and Jagged’s real-life dog to craft the character of Zeus. This artistic fusion reflects Ndhaus’s deep admiration for Furie’s distinctive style and Boys Club comics.

Today, Zeus is widely acknowledged within the crypto community as Pepe’s Dog and Matt Furies alter ego. Zeus is a dog that draws a direct connection to Matt. He embodies the large global community that marvels and support the works of Furie.

Zeus, Pepe’s Dog, is the ultimate tribute to the life-changing impact Matt’s work has made on the crypto space and people worldwide. Making Zeus a one-of-a-kind canine and a digital manifestation of Matt Furie himself.
